Ben Abramowitz is a 2021 Computing Innovation Fellow and Postdoctoral Researcher at Tulane University. Previously, he completed his PhD in Computer Science at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute advised by Dr. Elliot Anshelevich. In 2020, Ben was a visiting student at the Weizmann Institute of Science working with Ehud Shapiro’s crypto-democracy research group. Ben’s primary research interests lie in computational social choice, algorithmic game theory, and mechanism design.
